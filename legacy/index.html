About Us

6+ Years Experience

Specializing in civil litigation, family law, conveyancing, and more, with a proven track record in Kiambu County.

Jane Nyambura Mwangi

Principal Advocate & Founder

With over 6 years of legal experience, Jane Nyambura Mwangi founded Nyambura Mwangi & Co Advocates in 2023 with a vision to provide exceptional legal representation. She specializes in civil litigation, family law practice, conveyancing, succession, labour law, human rights, and sexual and gender-based violence matters. Jane holds a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from the University of Nairobi (Second Class Honours, 2016) and a Post Graduate Diploma in Law from the Kenya School of Law (2019). She was admitted to the bar as an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya on 4th July 2019 (Adm No. P105/16633/2019).
